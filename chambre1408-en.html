Overview

The Dolphin Hotel exists since 1895 and welcomes guests from all around the world. When you arrived, there were no rooms available so the maître d’hôtel decided to give you the room n°1408, the only room available this day. Rumors are spreading about some creepy events happening there: that if locked inside once, you never find your way out from it. “But why should we believe all these rumors?” you thought. Besides, you do not have any other alternative today!

Once inside, you realise that rumours were not lies, that a deep secret lies within this place. You need to get out quickly! But to escape, you must find an exit, which is difficult when you completely lose the sense of reality!
